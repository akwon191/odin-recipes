Sushi Recipe

Description

Making sushi at home is surprisingly simple. We'll start with how to make sushi for beginners, which means sushi rolls.

Ingredients

  • Bamboo rolling mat
  • Plastic wrap
  • Sharp knife
  • Seaweed
  • Sushi Rice
  • Fish of choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Place the bamboo rolling mat on a cutting board so the bamboo strips are running horizontally to you.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the seaweed.
  3. Add your ingredients toward the center of the rice-covered nori.
  4. Gently lift the bottom of the mat up and over the sushi. Press and shape the ingredients into a tube.
  5. Roll until just an inch of nori shows at the top. Seal the edge of the nori with a little cold water. Firm it up by squeezing the mat around the roll until it feels uniformly snug.
  6. Slice the sushi roll in half with a sharp knife on a cutting board. Then cut each half into thirds -- so you have 6 pieces of sushi.
  7. Line the slices of sushi up on platters or sushi plates.
